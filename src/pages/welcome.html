Welcome to Svalinn

Our extension was designed keeping your privacy and security as our top priority. Svalinn helps you manage access to websites securely.

How to Use:

  • Set up your Master Password during registration.
  • Use the extension popup to lock specific website URLs.
  • Access locked websites by entering your Master Password.

By clicking "Agree and Register", you agree to use Svalinn responsibly and understand that keeping your Master Password safe is crucial, as losing it may result in loss of access to locked websites.
